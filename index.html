David Robinson is inducted into the Naismith Memorial Basketball Hall of Fame.

Bio

  • Born: August 5, 1965 in Key West, FL
  • College: US Naval Academy (1983-1987)
  • Military: US Navy (1983-1989).
  • Rank: Lieutenant (junior grade)
  • NBA Draft: 1987. 1st round, 1st pick.
  • Team: San Antonio Spurs (1989-2003)
  • Team: USA (1986, 1987, 1988, 1992 (Dream Team), 1996)
  • Position: Center

College Achievements

  • NCAA Tournament Elite Eight (1986)
  • NCAA Player of the Year (1987)
  • NCAA Blocks Leader (1986, 1987)
  • NCAA Rebounds Leader (1986)
  • First Team All-American(1987)
  • Second Team All-American(1986)
  • CAA Player of the Year(1985-1987)
  • All-CAA First Team (1985-1987)
  • CAA All-Defensive Team (1987)
  • CAA Rookie of the Year (1984)
  • Wooden "Keys to Life" Award (2004)
  • NCAA Silver Anniversary Award (2012)

Team USA

  • Gold Medal: 1986 World Championships
  • USA Basketball Male Athlete of the Year(1986)
  • Silver Medal: 1987 Pan-American Games
  • Bronze Medal: 1988 Olympics
  • Gold Medal: 1992 Olympics (Dream Team)
  • Gold Medal: 1996 Olympics

NBA Achievements

  • NBA Champion (1999, 2003)
  • NBA MVP (1995)
  • NBA All-Star (1990-1996, 1998, 2000-2001)
  • All-NBA First Team (1991, 1992, 1995, 1996)
  • All-NBA Second Team (1994, 1998)
  • All-NBA Third Team (1990, 1993, 2000, 2001)
  • Defensive Player of the Year (1992)
  • All-Defensive First Team (1991, 1992, 1995, 1996)
  • All-Defensive Second Team (1990, 1993, 1994, 1998)
  • IBM Award (1990-1991, 1994-1996)
  • Sportsmanship Award (2001)
  • J. Walter Kennedy Citizenship Award(2003)
  • Scoring Champion (1994)
  • Rebounding Leader (1991)
  • Blocks Leader (1992)
  • Rookie of the Year (1990)
  • 50th Anniversary Team (1997)
  • 75th Anniversary Team (2022)
  • Sports Illustrated Sportsman of the Year (2003) w/ Tim Duncan
  • 2003: NBA names Community Assist Award "David Robinson Plaque"
  • February 17, 1994: Quadruple-Double: 34 points, 10 rebounds, 10 assists, and 10 blocks vs. Detroit Pistons.
  • April 24, 1994: Scored 71 points vs. LA Clippers

Halls of Fame

  • Naismith Basketball Hall of Fame (2009, 2010*)
  • US Olympic Hall of Fame (2008, 2009*)
  • FIBA Hall of Fame (2013, 2017*)

*-As a member of the Dream Team

Off the Court

  • 1987: Graduated from Naval Academy with BS in Mathematics
  • 1987-1989: Served in Civil Engineering Corps as part of Naval service.
  • 2001: Founded Carver Academy (now called IDEA Carver)
  • 2004: Became part-owner of the San Antonio Spurs
  • 2008: Founded Admiral Capital Group
  • 2011: Earned Masters of Arts in Administration from University of Incarnate Word
  • 2018: Board Member of V Foundation for Cancer Research
  • Co-owner of Jaguar Land Rover dealer in San Juan, TX
